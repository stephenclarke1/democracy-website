Democracy in Aotearoa

Democracy plays a huge part in how Aotearoa New Zealand gets things done. It is important for a nation to have a system of democracy that represents people, so that the descisions made by those in power reflect the attitudes of the majority of people.

The House of Representatives

In order to represent New Zealand's views, 120 people are elected to the House of Representatives, more commonly known as Parliament. 72 of those people are representatives of a particular area, or electorate.
