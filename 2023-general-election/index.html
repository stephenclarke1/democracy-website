2023 General Election

Even though you can't vote this election, it's useful to find out about this election anyway.

Check your enrolment

Because you're 18 or older, you are elegible to vote (and legally have to be enrolled). Be sure to check or update your details before the 10th of September to recieve an EasyVote card, which makes your voting experience easier.

If you are 18 on or before the 14th of October, you are elegible to vote in the upcoming election. Regardless of your age, you can apply to be automatically enrolled on your 18th birthday.
