2023 General Election

Even though you can't vote this election, it's useful to learn about it anyway.

Check your enrolment

Because you're 18 or older, you are elegible to vote (and legally have to be enrolled).

Be sure to check or update your details before the 10th of September to recieve an EasyVote card, which makes your voting experience easier.

If you are 18 on or before the 14th of October, you are elegible to vote in the upcoming election. Regardless of your age, you can apply to be automatically enrolled on your 18th birthday.

Election Information

Advance (postal) voting opens on the 2nd of October, as per the election timetable. The final day for voting is the 14th of October.

Party Information

This list has a bunch of cool services that aim to educate you on what each of the main parties stand for:

  • RNZ has a useful guide to party policy, which takes you through all the policies of the major parties.
  • Policy.nz is another great tool that gives you all of the parties policies in a concise manner.
  • Votecompass is TVNZ's survey that aligns your values with those of the parties.
