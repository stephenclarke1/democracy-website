Te Tai Tonga

Te Tai Tonga is your electorate. This page contains some resources specifically for your area, which is people who are on the Māori roll and live in Te Waipounamu and parts of Te Whanganui-a-Tara.

What are their policies?

There are many ways of finding out a candidate's policies. Many candidates hold events where you can meet and have a chat with them, which is a good way of finding out what they stand for. If they are party aligned, their party's policies are a good starting point.

You can also view your candidates' personal statements on policy.nz.
